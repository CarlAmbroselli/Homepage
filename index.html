Purpose

I'm an engineer who gets joy out of solving hard problems. I love minimalism, antifragility and writing code.

Personality

I'm highly rational, almost always positive and very structured in how I approach everything. I prefer honest communication and value feedback of any kind. I'm an ESTJ personality.

Communication

For organizational topics I prefer asynchronous communication (slack, email, text). The time I take to respond usually depends on priority, so if you need a response by a certain time, please include that information in your message.

For ideation, debates and chatter I prefer scheduled synchronous communication (i.e. I don't like asynchronous social "how are you" communication).
